InsuranceDAO.World - 去中心化保险

Peer-to-Peer Smart Insurance Mechanism Powered By Advanced Algorithm and AI.

通过创新算法和透明AI定价机制，彻底革新优化方式。
Revolutionizing optimization through innovative algorithms and transparent AI pricing mechanisms.

InsuranceDAO.World 将 拉格朗日优化算法 (Lagrange Optimization Algorithm) 的精确性与先进的 AI智能体 (AI agents) 相结合，为去中心化保险树立新标准。我们的AI驱动系统从链上和链下源收集市场情报，分析社区共识，并实时自动调整定价策略。这确保了每个保费都反映当前市场条件和风险因素。通过考虑质押金额、利率、期限、合约地址和市场波动性等变量，我们的平台动态校准保费——在投资者可负担性和保险提供商的公平回报之间取得完美平衡。

本项目从产品架构、智能合约开发、前端设计到AI算法接入均由本人在三个月内独立完成，基于 Next.js 框架 构建，并结合 Solana 区块链部署。
This project was independently developed by me within three months—from system architecture and smart contracts to frontend design and AI integration—using the Next.js framework and deployed on the Solana blockchain.

To You Who Read Till Here

17岁那年，我放弃了成为飞行员的机会，转身投向更广阔的商业世界。
At 17, I turned down the chance to become a pilot to pursue a broader commercial world.

19岁，我感受到功能性消费品风口，选择休学创业，成为中国最早将抗衰老成分与潮流饮品结合的探索者之一。
At 19, I sensed a wave in functional consumer goods, dropped out to found a startup, and became one of the earliest explorers combining anti-aging ingredients with fashion beverages in china.

23岁，在剑桥大学宿舍里，我与一个腾讯的朋友独立开发了一个 MVP —— 一个结合 AI 数字超模与过季时尚的线上奥特莱斯平台。
At 23, from my dorm at Cambridge, I built an MVP with a friend from Tencent for an AI-powered digital outlet combining virtual supermodels with off-season fashion.

我先后在证券、私募股权、风险投资等机构实习，积累了投资分析、财务建模与量化研究经验。
I’ve interned at securities firms, private equity, and venture capital institutions, building experience in investment analysis, financial modeling, and quantitative research.

我的优势在于：能用产品视角理解金融逻辑，用技术手段解决商业问题。
My strength lies in understanding financial logic from a product perspective and solving business problems with technical solutions.

疫情期间，我也参与了一个法律科技创业项目，主导 SaaS 产品架构设计。
During the pandemic, I also joined a legal-tech startup where I led the SaaS product architecture design.

而作为一名帆船运动员，我学会了在风浪中读风识向、保持冷静。这种能力，也成了我在变化时代中的生存逻辑。
As a sailor, I’ve learned to read the wind and stay calm amid turbulence — a survival logic I apply in an ever-changing world.

我相信，金融、技术与现实的融合，将创造新的秩序。
I believe finance, technology, and reality will converge to form a new order.

而我愿意成为那个连接者、设计者、实践者。
And I aspire to be the connector, the designer, and the builder of that vision.

我坚信，年轻人应该勇于表达观点、勇于执行，在市场与现实中汲取反馈与失败。
Young people should execute boldly and learn through real-world feedback, even failure.

这是探索自我、实现稳定成长的必经之路，也让我更加珍视每一个可能的机会。
This journey of self-exploration and steady growth has taught me to cherish every possible opportunity.

我的目标，是成为一个真正热爱世界、为世界创造价值的人，而非随波逐流的机会主义者。
My goal is to be someone who truly loves the world and creates value for it — not a drifting opportunist.
